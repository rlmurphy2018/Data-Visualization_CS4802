Hubway Bikeshare Data Exploration

The graphs below enable you to explore Hubway's data, as made available for a data challenge. Click a bubble in the bubble chart to update the bar and pie graph with station-specific information. The bubble chart on the left represents three days worth of trip data, sorted by the stations where bikes were rented from. The colors correspond to the different areas around Boston where the stations are located. If you click on a station, the bar and pie charts will update to show trip activity for the specific station.
